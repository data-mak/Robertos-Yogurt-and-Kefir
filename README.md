Project Overview

Roberto’s Ice Cream is a small, community-focused ice cream brand dedicated to creating high-quality, handcrafted frozen desserts using fresh ingredients and unique flavor combinations.

The company has significant amounts of data on its sales, marketing efforts, operational efficiency, product offerings that have been previously underutilized. This project thoroughly analyzes and synthesizes this data to uncover critical insights that will improve Roberto's Ice Cream success.

Insights and recommendations are provided on the following key areas:
•	Subcategory Level Performance: An analysis of Roberto’s various subcategory lines, understanding their impact on sales and returns.
•	Sales trend analysis: Comparing previous and current years dollar growth over year and what are the drivers for its growth.
•	Promotional Level Performance: An assessment of the promotional program on sales.
